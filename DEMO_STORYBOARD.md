## Visual continuity

- Record at 1440 × 900 and 100% zoom.
- Use the light theme and a visible, steady cursor.
- Keep the replay disclosure visible in the opening shot.
- Do not show account chrome, developer tools, secrets, or private live-analysis screens.
- Use straight cuts only; the product motion supplies enough visual continuity.
