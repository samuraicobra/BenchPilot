# BenchPilot final recording checklist

## Before recording

- [ ] Open the public demo URL in a signed-out fresh browser profile.
- [ ] Set the viewport to 1440 × 900 at 100% zoom.
- [ ] Hide bookmarks, extensions, notifications, downloads, and unrelated tabs.
- [ ] Clear `benchpilot:experiment-workspace:v1` from local storage and reload.
- [ ] Confirm the opening says **Public Build Week demo · validated replay**.
- [ ] Confirm the disclosure says live analysis is disabled on the public deployment.
- [ ] Rehearse the shot list once without recording.
- [ ] Record system audio separately only if it cannot be kept clean.

## During recording

- [ ] Follow `DEMO_SHOT_LIST.md` in order.
- [ ] Keep the cursor away from evidence text while speaking.
- [ ] Pause briefly on 1.692 V, approximately 1.100 V, and the uncertainty warning.
- [ ] Show the simulated label before applying the matrix update.
- [ ] Do not imply that the -0.460 V minimum proves reversal.
- [ ] Do not say acrylic binder caused the improvement.
- [ ] Show the report dialog and **Print / save PDF** control without opening OS dialogs in the main take.

## After recording

- [ ] Trim to 90–120 seconds; target 105 seconds.
- [ ] Add `DEMO_CAPTIONS.srt` and verify every cue against the final edit.
- [ ] Burn in captions or confirm the hosting platform enables them by default.
- [ ] Export 1080p H.264 with readable text and no cursor trails.
- [ ] Watch the exported file from beginning to end with audio off and then on.
- [ ] Verify zinc-air, manganese dioxide, KOH, hypothesis, and falsifiable captions.
- [ ] Keep one local master and one upload copy.
