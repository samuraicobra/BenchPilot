## Capture standards

- Primary viewport: 1440 × 900 at 100% zoom; mobile: 390 × 844.
- Capture only the application viewport—no account identity, extensions, developer tools, or unrelated tabs.
- Use the public replay deployment, never the private live-analysis Site.
- Keep the replay disclosure visible in the opening image.
- Do not crop provenance badges, simulated labels, chart legends, or uncertainty warnings.
- Export PNG in sRGB and confirm text remains readable at half size.

No screenshot automation was available during the release session, so filenames are reserved but image files must be captured manually from the prepared public states.
